My Research & Publication

Thesis Book

Deep Learning & Computer Vision

Automated Classification, Detection, and Segmentation of Guava Diseases using Deep Learning

People often refer to guava (Psidium guajava) as the "poor man's apple" or the "apple of the tropics." The leaves and fruit of guava are both very healthy because they are high in potassium, fiber, antioxidants, and vitamin C. Guava is very good for your health because it has nutrients that help your immune system, protect you from infections, improve digestion, keep your heart healthy, and lower high blood pressure. Guava does well in both tropical and subtropical places. Guava fruits and leaves are prone to various diseases that diminish yield, causing economic losses for farmers and health hazards for the populace.

This research presents an automated system using deep learning techniques (convolutional neural networks and segmentation architectures) to accurately classify, detect, and segment guava leaf and fruit diseases, offering a vital decision-support tool for farmers to protect yields and mitigate economic losses.
